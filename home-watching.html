Professional Home Watch Services

Whether you're a snowbird escaping the Arizona summer, a frequent traveler, or simply away for an extended period, your home deserves professional attention in your absence.

Our certified home-watching professionals conduct regular inspections to identify and address potential issues before they become costly problems:

  • Water leaks and plumbing issues
  • HVAC system malfunctions
  • Pest infestations
  • Security concerns
  • Storm damage
  • General maintenance needs

After each visit, you'll receive a detailed report with photos, so you always know the status of your property.

What We Check During Each Visit

Plumbing & Water

Check for leaks, run faucets, flush toilets, inspect water heater

HVAC Systems

Verify AC/heating operation, check thermostat settings, replace filters

Security Check

Inspect doors, windows, garage, and alarm systems

Electrical Systems

Test lights, check breaker panel, verify smoke detectors

Pool & Spa

Monitor water levels, check equipment, coordinate with pool service

Pest Inspection

Look for signs of pests, coordinate with exterminators if needed
