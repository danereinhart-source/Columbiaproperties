Frequently Asked Questions

Everything Phoenix landlords want to know before hiring a property manager.

How much does property management cost in Phoenix?

Columbia Properties charges a transparent flat fee with no hidden charges. For 1 to 4 unit properties it's 8% of monthly rent, with tenant placement and lease renewal fees clearly disclosed on our pricing page. We offer discounted pricing for larger multifamily portfolios, so just reach out for a custom quote. Most Phoenix managers charge 8 to 12% plus a menu of add-on fees, so we keep it simple and clear upfront.

Do you have a price-match guarantee?

Yes. We'll match and beat any competitor's pricing. If you're currently with another manager, see our switch page for how we make the transition seamless.

How long does it take to find a tenant?

Most properties we manage are leased within 2 to 4 weeks. We use professional photography, listings syndicated across all major platforms (Zillow, Apartments.com, Zumper, and more), and proactive outreach to minimize your vacancy time.

How do you screen tenants?

We run comprehensive background checks including credit reports, criminal history, eviction records, income and employment verification, and prior landlord references, all while staying fully compliant with fair housing law. We only place tenants we'd trust in our own properties.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Phoenix metro, including Scottsdale, Mesa, Tempe, Gilbert, Chandler, Queen Creek, Peoria, Glendale, Surprise, Goodyear, and Cave Creek. We also manage properties in Tucson and Northern Arizona (Sedona, Flagstaff, Prescott, and Payson).

Do you handle evictions?

Yes. Our thorough screening minimizes the need, but when necessary we manage the full process: serving the proper notices, court filing, and coordination with the Maricopa County justice court system from start to finish, in full compliance with Arizona law.

Can I switch from my current property manager?

Absolutely, and we make it seamless. We coordinate directly with your current manager, handle all paperwork and tenant communication, and ensure zero disruption to your rental income during the switch. Learn more on our switch page.

What is the free Rental Market Analysis?

It's a complimentary, no-obligation report that evaluates current rental rates, comparable properties, demand trends, and your property's optimal price point, plus a custom management roadmap. Request yours here.

Do you offer home watching for seasonal residents?

Yes. Our Home Watching service is popular with snowbirds and out-of-state owners who need professional oversight of their Arizona property while they're away, with regular documented check-ins and fast response to any issues.

Do I get an owner portal?

Yes. Every owner gets 24/7 access to an online portal to view financial statements, track maintenance requests, review lease documents, and monitor your property's performance in real time.

How and when do I get paid?

We collect rent online and disburse owner funds on a consistent monthly schedule, along with a clear monthly statement so you always know exactly where your money is going.

Who are the people behind Columbia Properties?

Columbia Properties is led by two licensed Arizona brokers with institutional real estate backgrounds and decades of combined experience managing single-family and multifamily properties. Meet the team on our about page.
