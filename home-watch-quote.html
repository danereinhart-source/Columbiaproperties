What We Check on Every Visit

A consistent, documented walkthrough of your home, inside and out, so problems are caught early while you're away.

1

Water & Plumbing

Check for leaks, run fixtures, inspect the water heater, and watch for the damage a slow leak causes in an empty house.

2

HVAC & Electrical

Verify cooling and heating, confirm thermostat settings, test lights and smoke detectors, replace filters as needed.

3

Security & Storm

Inspect doors, windows, garage, and alarms, and check for monsoon or storm damage after major weather.

4

Pool, Pests & Vendors

Monitor pool levels and equipment, look for pests, and coordinate any service or repair on your behalf.
