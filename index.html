Common Questions

Phoenix Property Management FAQ

Everything you want to know before hiring a property manager in Phoenix.

How much does property management cost in Phoenix?

Columbia Properties charges a transparent flat fee with no hidden charges. See our full pricing page. Most Phoenix managers charge 8–12% of monthly rent plus a menu of add-on fees — we keep it simple and clear upfront.

How long does it take to find a tenant?

Most properties we manage are leased within 2–4 weeks. We use professional photography, listings syndicated across all major platforms, and proactive outreach to minimize your vacancy time.

What areas of Phoenix do you serve?

We serve the entire Phoenix metro — East Valley (Scottsdale, Mesa, Tempe, Gilbert, Chandler, Queen Creek), West Valley (Peoria, Glendale, Surprise, Goodyear), and Central/North Valley (Phoenix, Ahwatukee, Anthem, Cave Creek).

Do you handle evictions?

Yes. Our thorough screening minimizes the need, but when necessary we manage the full process — 5-day notices, court filing, and coordination with the Maricopa County system from start to finish.

What is a free Rental Market Analysis?

Our analysis evaluates current rental rates, comparable properties, demand trends, and your property's optimal price point — a full snapshot of how to maximize your return, at no cost or obligation.

Do you offer home watching for seasonal residents?

Yes. Our Home Watching service is popular with snowbirds who need professional oversight of their Phoenix property while they're away — regular check-ins and fast response to any issues.

Can I switch from my current property manager?

Absolutely. We make the transition seamless — we coordinate with your current manager, handle all paperwork and tenant communication, and ensure zero disruption to your rental income during the switch.

How do you screen tenants?

We run comprehensive background checks including credit reports, criminal history, eviction records, employment verification, and prior landlord references. We only place tenants who meet our rigorous qualification standards.

Do I have access to an owner portal?

Yes. Every owner gets 24/7 access to our online portal where you can view financial statements, track maintenance requests, review lease documents, and monitor your property's performance in real time.
