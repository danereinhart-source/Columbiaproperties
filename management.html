What We Offer as the #1 Property Management Company in Arizona

Marketing & Advertising

Leveraging a combination of traditional and digital channels, we ensure maximum visibility for each property under our management. Our advanced property management software system syndicates listings on top advertising platforms such as Apartments.com, Apartment List, Zillow, Zumper, and even Craigslist, to showcase our listings in front of millions of prospective renters.

Leasing & Tenant Screening

No more filling your days with countless prospect phone calls or wasting your afternoons commuting to showing after showing at your property. Let us handle that. While we're at it, we'll screen each applicant using our integrated software that seamlessly collects applications and fees before initiating criminal background screenings and credit checks for each applicant.

Rent Collections

Allowing tenants to pay rent online increases collection rates and speeds up payment processing time, equalling more money in your pocket sooner. Making bank runs to deposit checks is a thing of the past. Our integrated software features a "resident portal" that allows tenants to pay their rent online by linking a bank account or credit card.

Accounting & Financial Reporting

With our meticulous financial oversight, we ensure your investment operates efficiently, so each line item, from application fees to rent payments to vendor invoices, is accounted for and optimized to maximize your net income. We handle every aspect of your property's cash inflows and outflows, including payment processing, vendor management, and detailed expense tracking and categorization. We provide you with regularly generated financial reports, providing clear and concise overviews of your property's financial health.

Maintenance & Work Orders

Let us handle the maintenance headaches, so your tenants can enjoy a comfortable and well-maintained living space. Our online portal empowers tenants to submit work orders quickly and conveniently, 24/7. Once submitted, our team promptly reviews requests and assigns qualified vendors to address repairs efficiently. Tenants receive updates on the work order status throughout the process, keeping them informed and reducing the burden of you having to answer "when will this be fixed?" texts every day.

Fair Housing & Property Law Compliance

Don't let fair housing or property law uncertainties expose your investment to risk. Our property management services prioritize minimizing your liability by ensuring strict adherence to relevant regulations. Our team stays current on fair housing laws and state property management regulations, safeguarding your investment from legal issues. We implement transparent and compliant practices throughout the leasing process.
